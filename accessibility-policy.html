Our Commitment to Accessibility

At I Locksmith, we believe that everyone deserves equal access to information and services. We are committed to providing a website and service experience that is accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities.

We strive to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards and continuously work to improve the accessibility of our digital presence and garage door services.
