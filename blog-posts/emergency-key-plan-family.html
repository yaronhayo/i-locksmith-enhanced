Creating an Emergency Key Plan for Your Family

3 min read
I Locksmith South Bend
Updated Today

An emergency key plan ensures your family can access the home safely during urgent situations. Whether it’s a fire, a medical emergency, or being locked out, having a clear strategy provides peace of mind and quick solutions. Here’s how to create a reliable emergency key plan for your household.

1

Assign a Trusted Key Holder

Designate one or two family members or close neighbors as official key holders. They should be easily reachable and reliable during emergencies.

2

Use Secure Key Storage

Invest in a high-quality lockbox or smart key safe placed in a discreet but accessible area. Avoid hiding keys under doormats or flowerpots.

3

Create a Family Communication Plan

Ensure all family members know who to contact and where to go if they are locked out or if an emergency arises. Share phone numbers and backup contacts.

4

Update Keys Regularly

Revisit your emergency key plan yearly. Replace worn-out keys, reassign key holders if needed, and update storage methods for better security.
