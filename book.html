Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about booking our locksmith services

How quickly can you respond to an emergency lockout?

expand_more

For emergency lockouts, we typically arrive within 15-30 minutes in South Bend and surrounding areas. Our mobile locksmiths are strategically positioned throughout Northern Indiana to ensure the fastest possible response times for urgent situations.

Do you provide upfront pricing before starting work?

expand_more

Yes, absolutely! We believe in transparent pricing. Our locksmith will assess your situation and provide a clear, upfront quote before beginning any work. There are no hidden fees or surprise charges - you'll know exactly what you'll pay before we start.

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), and debit cards. Payment is due upon completion of service. For your convenience, we also accept contactless payments and mobile payment apps.

Do I need to be present during the service call?

expand_more

Yes, for security and legal reasons, the property owner or authorized person must be present during the service. We require proper identification and verification of ownership or authorization before performing any locksmith services on residential or commercial properties.

Can you make keys for my car on-site?

expand_more

Yes! We carry advanced key cutting and programming equipment in our mobile service vehicles. We can create and program keys for most car makes and models right at your location, including transponder keys, key fobs, and proximity keys.

What areas do you service for appointments?

expand_more

We provide locksmith services throughout South Bend and Northern Indiana, including Mishawaka, Elkhart, Goshen, Plymouth, Warsaw, and surrounding communities. For locations outside our primary service area, please call us to confirm availability and scheduling.
